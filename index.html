01About

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Hari Sundaram and Prof. Varun Chandrasekaran. I started my PhD in Fall 2023.

My research lies in trustworthy machine learning and AI safety. I study how learning systems encode, retain, and propagate concepts or undesirable behaviors, and how these mechanisms can be controlled through unlearning, causal analysis, and safety-oriented interventions.

I am currently interested in machine unlearning, concept representations in generative models, and safety risks in multi-agent systems, especially settings where harmful behavior or hidden influence can persist, transfer, or emerge through interaction.

Before UIUC, I earned my B.S. in Physics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).
